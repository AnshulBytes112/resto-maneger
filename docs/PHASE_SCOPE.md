# PHASE_SCOPE - FINBOOKS Development Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP - Core Billing & POS
- Basic Menu Management (Add/Edit items)
- Simple POS Interface (Select items, Quick payment)
- Receipt Generation (PDF/Thermal)
- Essential Dashboard (Today's total sales)

## Phase 2: Inventory & Table Management
- Dine-in Table Layout & Status Tracking
- KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket) workflow
- Basic Inventory (Stock counts, low stock alerts)
- Supplier/Purchase Entry recording

## Phase 3: Analytics & GST Compliance
- Advanced Sales Reports (Weekly/Monthly)
- GST Tax Invoicing & GSTR summaries
- Employee Login & Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Customer CRM (Contact tracking, history)

## Phase 4: Scaling & Integrations
- Multi-branch support
- Cloud Sync & Offline mode
- SMS/Email notifications for bills
- Integration with third-party delivery platforms
